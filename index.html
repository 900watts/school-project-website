Noise Pollution Survey Report

This report summarizes the findings of a recent survey conducted to assess noise pollution levels in our community.

Survey Purpose

The purpose of the survey was to understand the sources of noise pollution, its impact on residents, and to gather suggestions for mitigation.

Methodology

We distributed questionnaires to residents, conducted interviews, and utilized sound level meters in various locations.

Findings

The survey revealed that traffic, construction, and loud music were the primary sources of noise pollution. Many residents reported disturbed sleep and increased stress levels due to noise.

Recommendations

We recommend community awareness campaigns, stricter noise regulations, and noise barriers in high-traffic areas.

Conclusion

Addressing noise pollution is essential for improving the quality of life in our community.
